About Lengo Health Programme

Lengo Health Programme (LeHP) is a national NGO registered in Kenya in 2013. LeHP's mandate is to mobilize resources locally and internationally to invest in community health empowerment, focusing on public health research, healthcare interventions, skill development, and socio-economic concerns impacting overall community health. The headquarters is in Kisumu City, Nyanza Region.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

LeHP envisions a society and a future with health-empowered individuals living in healthy communities and functioning comprehensive healthcare delivery systems.

Our Mission

To leverage community and other resources to enhance capacities and equip individuals and communities to make informed choices toward improved healthy lives and sustainable, community-driven initiatives.

Our Vision

A future with health-empowered individuals living in healthy communities and functioning, comprehensive healthcare systems.

Our Values

  • Caring Attitude
  • Responsiveness and Dynamism
  • Respectfulness
  • Integrity and Accountability
  • Partnerships and Advocacy
