About Lengo Health Programme

Lengo Health Programme (LeHP) is a national NGO registered in Kenya in 2013. LeHP's mandate is to mobilize resources locally and internationally to invest in community health empowerment.

Our Core Pillars

We envision a society with health-empowered individuals living in healthy communities with comprehensive healthcare systems.

Our Mission

To leverage community and other resources to enhance capacities and equip individuals and communities to make informed choices toward improved healthy lives and sustainable, community-driven initiatives.

Our Vision

A future with health-empowered individuals living in healthy communities and functioning, comprehensive healthcare systems.
