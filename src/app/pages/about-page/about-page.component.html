About Lengo Health Programme

At Lengo Health Programme (LeHP), we are a non-profit organization committed to improving health and well-being across communities. Our mission is to empower individuals through education, health awareness, and sustainable development initiatives that create long-lasting positive change.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

We aim to bring transformative change through collaboration, compassion, and sustainability. Our focus is on education, health, and community-driven programs that address pressing global health challenges.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities through sustainable development programs, focusing on education, healthcare, and social welfare initiatives for vulnerable populations.

Our Vision

We envision a society where every individual, regardless of background, has the opportunity to live a healthy, fulfilling life. A world with equal access to resources and opportunities for all.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Transparency and ethical conduct in all actions.
  • Collaboration: Working together with communities, partners, and volunteers to achieve common goals.
  • Compassion: A deep sense of empathy and making a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve.
  • Sustainability: Focusing on long-term solutions to empower communities to thrive independently.
