About Us

Lengo Health Programme (LeHP) is a national NGO registered in Kenya in 2013. Our mission is to mobilize resources locally and internationally to enhance community health through public health research, interventions, and advocacy.

Download 2015 Breast and Cervical Report

Our Mission

LeHP seeks to leverage community resources to enhance capacities and equip individuals to make informed health choices, improving their quality of life.

Our Vision

LeHP envisions a society where individuals thrive in healthy communities with well-functioning healthcare systems.

Our Values

  • Caring Attitude: Ensuring community well-being.
  • Integrity and Accountability: Commitment to ethical practices.
  • Advocacy: Promoting health awareness and intervention.
  • Diversity and Teamwork: Inclusivity in all our efforts.

What We Do

We are committed to creating a tangible impact through community-focused initiatives. Below are a few of our key programs that are transforming lives across the globe.

Cervical Cancer Awareness & Screening

Our cervical cancer program focuses on educating communities about the risks, prevention methods, and early detection. We also offer screening services to ensure early diagnosis and save lives.
